About Me

I'm a computer science student at Nottingham Trent University and I am passionate about programming and technology.

My passion for programming started from a young age, when I was making games with a friend at school. This passion has lead me to pursue education. I studied at Software Engineering at Vision West Nottinghamshire and moved on to higher education by enrolling on a Computer Science course at Nottingham Trent where I am currently on my second year.

My passion for programming doesn’t stop at my studies, during the year I have tinkered and experimented with many other technologies such as the raspberry pi (Which my server is hosted on) and Arduino. These technologies allow me to learn about how hardware interacts with software and allows me to use software to interact with the real world.
